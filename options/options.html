Folder Organization

Consolidate Sparse Folders: Automatically moves bookmarks from folders with less than 3 bookmarks to their parent folders. This helps clean up your bookmark structure by eliminating folders that don't have enough content to justify their existence.

Example: If you have "Work > Projects > Old Project" with only 2 bookmarks, those bookmarks will be moved to "Work > Projects". If "Work > Projects" then has less than 3 bookmarks, they'll be moved to "Work".
